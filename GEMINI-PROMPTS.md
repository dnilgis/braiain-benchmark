# BRAIAIN — Gemini Image Generation Prompts
## Copy-paste each prompt into Google Gemini to generate the AI photos

> **Settings**: Ask Gemini to generate images at the highest quality. Save each as the filename listed. Aim for 800×600 or similar landscape ratio.
>
> **IMPORTANT**: After generating, download each image and upload it to your site's `/images/` folder. Then update the challenge JSON to point to `images/filename.jpg` instead of the Pollinations URL.
